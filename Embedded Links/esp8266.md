ESP8266: Low-power 32-bit MCU with integrated Wi-Fi.

1. 4 MB Flash, 80 KB User RAM, 36 KB IRAM;
2. (2.5 - 3.6)V application supply and I/Os;
3. 26 MHz crystal (external) / 52 MHz internal oscillator;
4. Integrated 802.11 b/g/n Wi-Fi;
5. SPI, I2C, I2S, UART;
6. 1 Timer;
7. 1 ADC (10-bit);
8. 1 GPIO, up to 16 general-purpose I/O pins;
9. JTAG, SDIO;
