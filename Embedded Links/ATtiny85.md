Low-Cost Small 8-bit AVR family 'tinyAVR' MCU

1. 8 kB EEPROM, 512 B SRAM
2. 4 Channel ADC 10-bit resolution
3. 6 GPIOs
4. 2 Timers
5. USI interface
6. Supply voltage 2.7V-5.5V
7. Operating temperature -40 C ... +85 C
