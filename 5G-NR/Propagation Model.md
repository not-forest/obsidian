Factors for channel impulse model:
- **path loss** - how much energy is lost between transmitter and receiver when signal is traveling in medium.
- **delay spread** - how much signal gets dispersed in time domain.
- **angle of arrival** - how signal changes based on the angle of receiver antenna.

> **Path Loss Exponent (n)** - shows how path loss increases with distance in **specific environment**:
> - free space: ~2;
> - urban areas: 2,7 - 3,5;
> - dense urban areas/ indoors: ~4;

> **Shadowing Factor (SF)** - loss caused by obstacles and other factors from environment: commonly in range **4 - 12 dB**
