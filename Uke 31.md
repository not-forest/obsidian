Jeg har dessverre <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">ikke noen</mark> bil - unfortunately i don't have any car.
Ingen har sett henne - no-one had ever saw her.
Han vil ikke se noen - he does not wants to see anyone.
Hun har ikke skrevet noen rapporter - she haven't written any reports

Han har ikke spist noe eple - he haven't eat any apple.
Vi har ikke noe te - we do not have any tea.
Hun har ikke noe - she does not have anything.
Jeg vil ikke kjøpe noe - i do not want to buy anything.
Jeg har ikke solgt noe - i haven't sold anything.

Jeg ser ingenting der - i do not see anything there.
Ingenting er rimelig i denne butikken - nothing is worth buying at that shop.

1. Øv deg
a) Jeg har ingen elegante sko. - i do not have any elegant shoes.
b) De har ingen venner her. - they have no friends there.
c) Jeg har ikke tatt noen bagasje med meg. - i haven't took any luggage with me.
d) Han har ikke noe sukker hjemme. - he has no sugar at home.
e) Ingen vil komme på festen - no one will come to the party.

2. Oversett
a) There are no good hotels in this town:
	Der finnes ikke noen gode hoteller i denne byen.
b) No-one can help me:
	Ingen kan hjelpe meg.
c) Nothing is happening here. It is so boring!
	Der skjer ingenting her. Der er så kjedelig!
d) She haven't bought any skirt, but i have bought many blouses:
	Hun har ikke kjøpt noe skjørt, men jeg har kjøpt mange bluser.
e) We do not have any money:
	Vi har ingen penger.
