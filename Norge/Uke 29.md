- Jeg skal gå på en fjelltur. - I wil go to a mountain tour.
c) Det er <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">noen</mark> kafeer i sentrum. - Are there any cafes in center?
d) Har du <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">noen</mark> kopp? - Do you have any cup?
e) Han leser <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">noe</mark> interessant i avisen. - He reads something interesting in the newspaper.
f) Mia fikk <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">noen</mark> venner på skolen. - Mia found some friends at school.

	Han spiser noen brødskiver ti

1. Overrsett
a)
- Hva er ditt neste reisemål? - What is your next travel goal?
- Jeg tror jeg sier ja til en pakketur. Jeg vil ikke booke billetter og overnatting på egen hånd. Jeg har ikke tid til det. - I think I'll say yes to a package tour. I don't want to book tickets and accommodation on my own. I don't have time for that.
b)
- Hvordan var vært i Spania? - How was it like in Spain?
- Det var veldig varmt, og sola skinte hele tida. Vi solte oss på stranda og badet i sjøen nesten hver dag. - It was very hot, and the sun was shining all the time. We sunbathed on the beach and swam in the sea almost every day.
c) 
- Hvor skal vi overnatte? - Where shall we overnight?
- Jeg har funnet et lite hotell i nærheten av skogen. Vi kan gå tur eller leie en sykkel. - I have found a small hotel close to us in the forest. We can go walking or rent a bike.
d) 
- Hva skal du gjøre i Frankrike? - What will you do in France?
- Jeg vil besøke noen venner og gå i museer. Dessuten vil jeg ta mange bilder. - I will visit some of my friend and go to the museums. Most importantly i will take a lot of pictures.
e) Hun liker å dra på sightseeing sammen med en guide. - She likes to go sightseeing together with a guide.
f) Det er dyrt å bo på et hotell. Denne sommeren skal jeg dra på campingtur. - It is expensive to live at the hotel. This sommer i shall go to a camping tour.

a) Do you have some sugar at home?

	Hun har noen store kofferten, men foretrekker å reise med en håndbagasje.
c) Three weeks ago we were visiting several friends in Barcelona.
	For tre uker siden besøkte vi noen venner i Barcelona.
d) Are there any traveling agencies in this city?
	Er det noen reisebyråer i denne byen?
e) I want to overnight at the hotel in the city center.
	Jeg vil overnatte på et hotell i sentrum.

Det skjer ofte at folk velger all inclusive-ferie, det vil si at de får en hel pakke med transport, rom og måltider. Nesten alt er betalt på forhånd, og du vil sjelden trenge din lommebok! Nordmenn besøker også Polen, og Sopot blir ofte et første valg. Sopot ligger ved sjøen, og det finnes en kjent brygge der. For nordmenn er prisene veldig rimelige. > Norwegians are very happy to travel to the South. They often book a package trip with flights and hotels. Some popular destinations are the Canary Islands and the areas around the Mediterranean. It often happens that people choose an all-inclusive holiday, that is, they get a whole package with transport, rooms and meals. Almost everything is paid in advance, and you will rarely need your wallet! Norwegians also visit Poland, and Sopot is often a first choice. Sopot is located by the sea, and there is a famous pier there. For Norwegians, the prices are very reasonable. In addition, many of them tend to visit a dentist's office or hair salon in Poland. This is precisely because of the price differences between the countries.
