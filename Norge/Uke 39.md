1. Øv deg
a) Jeg har en venn som bor i Italia. - I have a friend that lives in Italy.
b) Hun gikk hjem fordi det ble sent. - She went home because was late.
c) Han tror at det kommer til å regne - She think that it will rain.
d) Hun spør om jeg har litt tid. - She asked whether i have little bit of time.
e) Jeg skal vaske bilen dersom jeg får tid. - I shall wash the car if i will get time.
f) De vil dra på fest selv om de er trøtte. - They want to go to the party even though they are thirsty.
g) Hun pleier å lese mens hun spiser - She usually reads while she eats.
h) Han leser mye så han er forberedt til eksamen. - He reads a lot so he is prepared for the exam.
i) Han må skynde seg fordi bussen går snart - He must hurry up, because the bus goes fast.
j) De sier at de trives på landet. - They are saying that they are happy in the village.
k) Han kom på jobben till tross for at han var syk - He came to work despite the fact that he was sick.
